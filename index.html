Tire Pressure Optimizer

Incorrect tire pressure reduces fuel economy, accelerates tire wear, and compromises handling. Find your ideal PSI based on conditions and your vehicle type. Optimal inflation extends tire life by up to 25% and improves fuel economy by up to 3%.

💡 Pro Tip from Jimmy
Check tire pressure when cold — before driving more than 1 mile. Warm tires read 4–6 PSI higher than actual cold pressure.

Oil Life & Change Estimator

Modern engines have tight tolerances that depend on clean, viscous oil. Knowing exactly when your oil needs changing — based on YOUR driving habits — saves money and prevents serious engine damage. Over 40% of engine wear happens with degraded oil.

💡 Pro Tip from Jimmy
Short trips under 5 miles are harder on oil than long highway drives. City driving should trigger earlier changes than mileage alone suggests.

Brake Health Analyzer

Brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. This analyzer uses your driving patterns and symptoms to estimate brake pad remaining life and urgency. Ignoring brake wear can turn a $200 pad replacement into a $1,000+ rotor repair.

⚠️ Safety Warning
Grinding, squealing, or pulling during braking requires immediate professional inspection. Do not delay safety-critical repairs.

Fuel Efficiency Calculator

Calculate your true MPG, monthly fuel costs, and identify how maintenance factors affect your fuel economy. Regular tune-ups, proper tire inflation, and clean air filters can improve MPG by 15–25%. See exactly what you could be saving.

💡 Pro Tip from Jimmy
A dirty air filter alone can reduce fuel economy by up to 10%. An annual tune-up pays for itself in fuel savings within months.
Annual Maintenance Cost Estimator

Understand what you should budget for your vehicle's maintenance based on age, mileage, and type. Proactive maintenance costs 3–4x less than reactive repairs. Plan smarter, never be surprised by your car again.

💡 Pro Tip from Jimmy
Vehicles over 100K miles need more attention to timing belts, coolant systems, and suspension. Budget 30% more per year after this milestone.
